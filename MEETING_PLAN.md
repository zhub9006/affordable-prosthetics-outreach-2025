# Meeting Planning — Outreach Coordinators

## Recommended Meeting Location: Nashville, Tennessee

Nashville is the geographic midpoint and most practical central hub for our three outreach team cities. It offers a wide variety of meeting venues, restaurants, and logistics infrastructure.

### Why Nashville?
| Factor | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Geographic Center** | Closest major city to the centroid of Chicago, Boston, and Atlanta |
| **Airport Access** | BNA (Nashville International) — major hub with direct flights from all three cities |
| **Accommodation** | Wide range of hotels near downtown and midtown |
| **Meeting Venues** | Numerous restaurants, cafes, and conference spaces |

---

## Meeting Venue Recommendations

### 1. The Southern Steak and Oyster ⭐ Top Pick
- **Type:** Restaurant (private dining available)
- **Location:** 3rd Avenue South, Downtown Nashville
- **Coordinates:** 36.1598, -86.7746
- **Why:** Upscale yet approachable, private dining rooms, central downtown location
- **Best for:** Formal meetings, monthly all-hands

### 2. Bongo Java
- **Type:** Cafe / Coffee Shop
- **Location:** Belmont Boulevard, Nashville (also Airport location)
- **Coordinates:** 36.1322, -86.7958
- **Why:** Casual, collaborative atmosphere; great for team standups
- **Best for:** Weekly standup meetings, informal discussions

### 3. Third Man Records
- **Type:** Event Space / Studio
- **Location:** 623 7th Avenue South, Downtown Nashville
- **Coordinates:** 36.1522, -86.7782
- **Why:** Unique, inspiring venue; good for creative brainstorming sessions
- **Best for:** Quarterly strategy sessions, team-building events

## Meeting Cadence

| Meeting Type | Frequency | Location | Format |
|-------------|-----------|----------|--------|
| **Weekly Standup** | Every Monday | Rotate between cities (Chicago → Boston → Atlanta) | Virtual or in-person |
| **Monthly All-Hands** | 1st Thursday/month | Nashville (The Southern Steak and Oyster or Bongo Java) | In-person preferred, virtual option |
| **Quarterly Strategy** | Quarterly | Nashville (Third Man Records or private dining) | In-person, full-day working session |

---

## Rotation Schedule (Weekly Standup)
1. **Month 1:** Chicago team hosts
2. **Month 2:** Boston team hosts
3. **Month 3:** Atlanta team hosts
4. Repeat cycle

---

## Logistics Tips
- **Nashville Parking:** Several garages downtown; Bongo Java has street parking
- **BNA Airport:** 8 miles from downtown; rideshare ~$20
- **Hotel Recommendations:** Downtown Nashville hotels near Broadway or Midtown (near Vanderbilt)
- **Group Dining:** The Southern Steak and Oyster can accommodate large groups with advance reservation
