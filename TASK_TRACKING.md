## How to Create a New Task
1. Go to the [Issues tab](https://github.com/zhub9006/affordable-prosthetics-outreach-2025/issues)
2. Click "New Issue"
3. Add labels: `team:city-name`, `type:category`, `priority:level`
4. Fill in the task template with details and deliverables
